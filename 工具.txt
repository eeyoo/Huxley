机械工具
==================
- Drill bits 钻头
- Precision screwdriver set 精密螺丝刀
- Allen keys, 1.5mm and 2.5mm 内六角扳手
- 10mm spanner(M6 nut) M6常用扳手
- 15cm adjustable spanner 15厘米可调扳手
- File 锉刀
- Half round needle file 半圆形针锉刀
- Craft knife 好刀一把
- Fine tweezers 好用镊子一把
- 300mm rule 30厘米长钢尺
- Vernier or digital callipers 数显游标卡尺
- Square 直角钢尺
- Fine nosed pliers 尖嘴钳
- Pliers 老虎钳
- Bench vice 台钳
- PTFE plumber's tape 胶带和尼龙扎带


电工工具
=================
- Digital Multimeter 数字万用表
- A fine-tipped soldering iron 高端电烙铁
- Precision screwdrivers 螺丝刀
- Solder(flux is also useful)焊锡（助焊剂）
- Hair dryer 热风机
- Wire strippers/cutters 电线割皮
- Scissors 剪子
